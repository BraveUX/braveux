Unbox

Thinking outside 
the inbox

Task-Based Email Application

We re-imagined how your inbox should work, then built an interactive prototype to test our design with real users.

Challenge

Fixing the problem of email

Lost emails. Lengthy chains. Long nights. We had to rethink how users manage and interact with their inboxes day-to-day.

Solution

An interactive prototype worth testing

Designed to organize and manage email so users can get past the hassle and truly communicate.

Scope

  • Information Architecture
  • Application Design

It’s a sprint, not a marathon

Rapid iteration sprints were our strategy; we immediately established the core product and polished as we learned.

Information nation

Having ideas doesn’t count when you can’t execute. We brought concepts to life through thoughtful information architecture decisions, raising the bar for a new way of application thinking.

Stay on task

Unbox separates items the user selects into a to-do list for them to complete. This dynamic way of looking at email gives the user actionable items from otherwise messy inboxes.

Playing tag, not catch-up

A tough challenge meets a simple solution. We use tags to help users keep track of everything in one place and monitor their workstreams.

Pick a card, any card

A fresh way to look at email. By placing critical information and emails on cards, the user can arrange and manage their inbox more efficiently.
