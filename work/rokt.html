Rokt

Solving the internet’s 
problem with ads

Ecommerce Advertising Platform

We helped Rokt wrangle its powerful algorithms and marketplace into a seamless suite, so that marketers, publishers and customers alike experience a better web.

Challenge

Teaching Marketing's 
Old Dogs New Tricks

A lot of experience, sophistication and nuance has gone into Rokt’s history of serving content users actually care about. How can users efficiently understand a complex, new way of thinking?

Solution

Ground the Data to 
See the Signal

We mapped a new mental model that aligns deep data concepts with the marketing objectives that users already know and use, amplified with Rokt’s new brand and extended to places like tablet and mobile.

Scope

  • Research
  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

How it works

A Publisher (like Ticketmaster) sells a ticket online.

Rokt finds a relevant Advertiser (like Uber) to show the customer an offered tailored to them, like a discounted ride to the concert.

Instead of distracting ads in the middle of the shopping experience, customers get optional, always-relevant offers, meaning everybody wins.

Getting the band
back together

Advertisers run Campaigns on Publisher's checkout screens. They rely on each other, but previously had to use completely separate tools. Now, they can see the right amount of data 'across the fence,' in one platform.

Forests, not trees

Creating a tangible, visual concept of abstract marketing goals was our top priority. Our cards keep users focused in a sea of data.

Embarking on a journey

A complex script of dynamic steps is simplified in a single view, where users can quickly map out paths, measure performance, and keep up with customer behavior.

Ready to go live

A strong emphasis on what-you-see-is-what-you get lets users actually see what their customers will see, in real time, so that every transaction is flawless.

A pulse on the
market from anywhere

Customer opinions can shift rapidly; deals can go viral and sell out. We identified the most urgent features for our mobile interface, saving last-minute hikes to the office.

Hearing it here first

Users are more than just transactions. Brave traveled to Sydney and NYC for weeks of in-depth interviews with users of all kinds, learning the success and friction of customers, account managers, and support teams.

A roadmap to rock

We worked closely with the Rokt team to create a new UX guide that will help future teams learn and extend the new UX framework.
