Patient Support Platform

We designed an online community where patients of rare conditions can get curated, medically-verified news and support from other patients, all in one place.

Challenge

What You Don't Know 
Could Kill You

For rare conditions, online searches invite dead ends, misinformation, and even potentially lethal remedies. And users are often searching on their own.

Solution

Curate and 
Congregate

We built an online community around an algorithm that prioritizes medically-accurate articles, tailored to each patient, that can be shared with specialists, loved ones and more.

Scope

  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

Getting to know you

With rare conditions, every patient's journey is unique. We created a behavior loop where users get immediate value, while training the algorithm along the way.

Head and heart

Learning about clinical trials doesn't have to feel clinical. We balanced credible resources with warm, comforting design cues.

A clear picture 
of health

On the road to recovery, every detail matters. We created an intuitive workflow that makes it easy for patients to keep track of their treatments, procedures, and visits.

Helping patients take control

Setting goals — and keeping them — is something any patient can do, to help take back control over their condition. Our UI balances inspirational objectives with customizable progress tracking.

From living room 
to waiting room

Learning about a condition shouldn't be left at home. Our responsive interfaces let patients bring the articles and issues that matter the most right to their doctor's ppointments, with easily verifiable sources.

Care that shares

When your condition is one in a million, finding local support is challenging. By providing ways for patients to share, react or follow content, we lay the groundwork for a positive, inclusive and welcoming environment.
