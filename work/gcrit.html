Design Critique Platform

We helped Gensler architect a platform for their designers to improve each other's projects from anywhere in the world, in-studio or on-the-go.

Challenge

Finding a Thousand Words for a Picture

Gensler works on the highest-profile projects, across oceans and timezones. How can a multidisciplinary process best integrate the diversity of views within?

Solution

Construct the Best Criticism

We fused modern standards for collaboration, commenting, and following with an engrossing visual-forward approach, all harmonized with Gensler's trademark sense of style.

Scope

  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

Down to the wire

In a world driven by deadlines, we highlighted which projects needed valuable input the most - and fast - so the creative process keeps running smooth

Breathtaking views

Generous, visual layouts let users really absorb the fine details of each project, because an informed critic is a better critic

Drawn together

Intuitive type and pen controls let reviewers get right to the heart of it. Color coding and type options help visualize revisions.

Conversation engaged

Reviewers can comment directly on individual parts of each project and even leave audio comments if they're in a rush, making collaboration possible for even the busiest people.

From pocket to drafting table

Inspiration can strike at any time, and our native app and desktop webapp focus on each strength: touch drawing and audio recording meets well-written comments and side-by-side comparison views.

A Work Mixer

The minds at Gensler take pride in their work. Profiles showcase an individual's best work, while helping put faces to names - for a higher caliber of collaborative discussion.
