Immersive Travel Platform

We designed an immersive travel platform that opens the doors to Bhutan, starting with a trip of our own to make sure the UX lived up to the real-life experience.

Scope

In the Field

To truly understand what it would be like to plan, prepare and embark on a journey to Bhutan, we did exactly that. The discovery, the stress, the joy — all of this informs not just what a user would expect, but what a user will truly need.

A good omen

We created a brand around Bhutan’s own good luck charm, the Gray Langur. From here, a complete system of tones, legends and symbols evolved, guiding the user and rounding out the experience.

Go on an adventure

Curated, once-in-a-lifetime experiences play out across 
our Magic Map, the centerpiece of our travel platform.

Every detail in 
one place

Users don’t have to deal with dozens of tabs, lists and spreadsheets anymore. A powerful, unified travel planner walks them through every step of the way.

Customs 
meets custom

Gone is a never-ending passport and customs form. Instead, we focus on what matters, in an order that makes sense to users, not diplomats.

A pocket guide

Users aren’t dragging their desktops to the airport. Our mobile UI shows everything from stops to who’s rooming with who.

Destination: 
headquarters

Gray Langur agents can tailor every last part of each trip through a powerful backend, that lets them dynamically respond to conditions on the ground.
