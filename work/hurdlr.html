Hurdlr

Self-empowerment 
for solopreneurs

Financial Tracking Platform

We helped Hurldr put powerful financial and tax tools to the busiest companies of all: the self employed.

Challenge

An Entire Business, 
Handheld

Ad-hoc spreadsheets are error-prone and messy, even when you have a full-screen desktop. But what happens when your desk is miles away?

Solution

Charity Meets 
Consistency

We developed a scaleable framework that highlights key financial metrics and quick actions when you're mobile, and context-rich analysis when you're at your desk.

Scope

  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

A Stress-Free Welcome

Diving into the world of self-employment finances can be hell. Our animated onboarding steps bring the process to life, while helping quickly communicate the power of Hurdlr - even if you're in a rush.

Mobile-First
Done Right

The mobile versions of many finance apps are cluttered at best and watered-down at worse. We knew we had to nail the right level of focus on mobile if the entire platform would succeed.

Do: Condense the Numbers

Careful type selections ensure that the backbone of finance - numerals - are easy to track, but compact enough to fit in a column, or a mobile phone.

Don't: Stretch the Math

When you don't balance function, it's easy to let form waste valuable space, a mistake many other finance apps make.

Desktops that Get to Work

Bigger isn’t always better. We had to find the right additional detail and context to show on bigger screen sizes.

Intuitive Facts from
Front to Back

Not everyone thinks the same way. A variety of carefully curated data formats helps patterns stand out.

Leveraging a Brand

Even though this version of Hurdlr features are new, many solopreneurs already trust Hurdlr with their finances. We optimized, evolved and extended their brand language, so that while everything feels fresh, it still feels familiar.
