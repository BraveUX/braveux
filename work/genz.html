Mentoring Social Platform

We designed a web application that links students and recent graduates with real professionals, giving careers a jump start.

Challenge

A fresh interface for both students and professionals

How do you get millennials and anyone over 35 to agree that something is cool?

Solution

A modern platform designed for all users

Weaving in the best of modern trends with smart, intuitive information brings everyone together.

Scope

  • Brand System
  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

Functional, as well as beautiful

We started with information architecture to establish functionality before applying visual design, making the interface as beautiful as it is usable.

An organized life for students

Students and recent graduates can find everything they need in a central location and via the universal navigation.

Attention to detail from every side

We ensure that mentors also feel at home in the interface and catered to their interests with relevant statistics and a custom scheduler.

Skimming by cards

The interface uses cards as an organization tool to improve skimmability. Users can quickly sort through information presented to them.
