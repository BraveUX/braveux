Hourly Jobs Search Application

We redesigned the mobile apps for Snagajob, helping connect hourly workers with employers who need them.

Challenge

Make piecemeal into a whole meal

We improved both brand consistency and communication between product, design, and development.

Solution

A top to bottom refresh

We went deeper than just the app screens. We uncovered process problems and helped Snagajob get back on course.

Scope

  • Employee Research
  • App Redesign
  • Pattern Library
  • UX Process Consulting

Unifying and updating the app design

Snagajob offers two apps — one for Job Seekers and one for Employers — for both Android and iOS. We refreshed and unified the designs for all four, creating a more holistic ecosystem.

Multiple operating systems

For both apps, we delivered compatible iOS and Android mockups, bringing the suite up to speed with iOS and Material Design standards.

All about the follow-through

We gave the employer app its own look, but kept the patterns and design building blocks the same, helping reinforce a consistent brand experience.

Looking at the bigger picture

We conducted interviews with the client’s internal teams and provided recommendations on workflow involving UX designers, product managers, engineers, and marketing reps.

Streamlined process from design to dev

In any united family of apps, the smooth handoff from mockups to development is key. We provided documentation in addition to a walkthrough of Sketch to Zeplin for the cleanest process possible.
