Interactive Data Visualization
`

40 American cities hold the secret to thriving communities. We designed a system to translate the laws that make it happen.

Challenge

Laws are in the weeds

Every city resident is affected by dozens of intersecting laws designed to hopefully improve their quality of life. But how do we evaluate which cities do this well?

Solution

A report card that gets to the point

A brand new medal system, with on-demand context and related cities help policymakers and residents alike quickly get the big picture.

Scope

  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design
  • Front End Development

The law, in a satellite view

An interactive map helps visualize not just where the best policies are, but how those policies affect their region.

Capturing the voice of a city

Every city in this report has a customized breakdown that highlights what it’s doing well, and more importantly, why—all backed by defensible research.

How does a policy affect an entire nation?

Whether it’s greener communities or quitting smoking, single issues have their own comprehensive view, across every major American city.

Medal rankings on the fly

A dynamic data visualization system means that medal counts, charts and graphs can be adjusted the moment laws go into effect—without needing a heavy redesign process.

Code that goes anywhere

We worked with a custom, law-analysis-based CMS to create extensible, maintainable code that will last for years of future reports.
