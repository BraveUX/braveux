Stretch Live

Bringing
Stretch to Life

Brand and Marketing Site

We set out to reposition Stretch to be more competitive in the Live Streaming space and ended up creating a fun, vibrant and effective brand that converts users into lifelong customers.

Challenge

A story that didn’t live
up to the experience

How could we transition the Stretch brand to be modern, engaging and appealing to a broad audience, without losing the soul of the company?

Solution

Keep the characters,
tell a new tale

Stretch was already loved by their customers for their incredible support. We shifted customer perceptions with a new, great product.

Scope

  • Brand Strategy
  • Brand System
  • Interaction Design
  • Front End Development

Taking Stretch Live

We renamed Stretch Internet to Stretch Live, changing a passive brand into an active experience.

Our mark is a fine line between literal and abstract: a recording light is universally understood as being “on air;” a play button and progress bar are hallmarks of online viewing; and the resulting solid line can infinitely bend, flex and support any message - just like their platform.

Standing Out in a Crowd

Our responsive brand system adapts to any interface or marketing need, with a responsive logo and system of brand elements, typography, and color. Just like The Stretch product itself, the brand site seamlessly transitions from feature to feature to tell a fluid story, a story which balances the emotional with the rational, providing credibility without sacrificing excitement.

Fighting Fires

We created fast, effective views that let users instantly zoom in on any technical issue, with supporting tools and historical data to help them find the right solution.

Aspire to Inspire

We set out to create an experience that made finding the best streaming solution engaging, fun, and - above all else - useful for our users.

We created a visually rich environment that helps each user envision their own events streaming on Stretch - all backed by facts that make decision-making easy.
