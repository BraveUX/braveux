Live Viewer Portal

We listened to viewers and gave them what they want: an intuitive, interactive live event viewer, from smartphone to smart TV.

Challenge

A Crowded Live 
Event Space

Most portals are chaotic, unrelated clips and misleading thumbnails. How can a modern viewer feel at home online?

Solution

Re-centering on 
Experience

Content now gets organized in a familiar way. And live events invite participation, so no viewer feels left out.

Scope

  • Research
  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

Mobile-first

The data couldn’t be clearer: mobile is where the future of streaming video is headed. For those craving a bigger experience, our fully-flexible UI delivers up to giant, HD screens.

Make it tangible

We found that users intuitively had an idea of what an ‘event’ was, so we turned this mental concept into an intuitive way to get around.

A personal touch

No one wants to miss out when something’s live. Our personal account system empowers each user to subscribe to what they love, and get timely reminders on what’s next.

Be immersive

Users can react to live events in real time. They can also browse through in-depth stats, and even make donations - whatever each event calls for.

For communities that scale

From sprawling athletic conferences to community events, all of the content is intuitive, dynamic, and easy to find.
