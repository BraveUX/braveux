Independent Journal Review

Redesigning your 
morning paper

Social-First, Mobile-First News Platform

We designed an interface for Independent Journal Review, a news publishing platform that averages over 40 million visitors each month.

Challenge

What is Independent Journal Review?

Social media drives more traffic to the Independent Journal Review platform than any other source. Our job was to decrease the drop-off rate and increase brand recognition.

Solution

A cut above the rest

The clean, cohesive, and fully responsive experience provides much needed clarity for the user and credibility for the publication.

Scope

  • Brand System
  • Information Architecture
  • Interaction Design

A lighter take on timely news

The previous platform was dark and difficult to navigate. We provided a new unified interface system that made reading bright, welcoming, and easy.

More than just a pretty face

We architected a layout to present stories by topic as a more engaging and intuitive way to group articles than the standard feed.

The story is the experience

We made the story king, keeping related items and share links on the side. Why? We want users to have the best content experience possible.

Putting more than a face to a name

An About section should answer not just “who?” but also “why?” It’s a testimony to the heart and culture of an organization. We included fun facts, stats, and plenty of photos to more effectively tell their story.

Mobile, desktop, and everything in between

We’ve got responsive on lock. Starting mobile-first, we architected a user experience that’s beautiful and functional from pocket to widescreen.
