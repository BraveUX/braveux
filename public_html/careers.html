UX Designer

We are looking for an experienced UX designer whose designs exude both beauty and brains.

Responsibilities include:

  • Designing brands, visual systems & complex interfaces.
  • Creating pixel-perfect mockups from wireframes with tidy organization.
  • Working on existing designs and applications to extend brands.
  • Work closely with strategists, developers and clients.
  • Staying ahead of design trends.

Must haves:

  • Proficient Adobe Illustrator skills.
  • Proficient Adobe Photoshop skills.
  • A diverse aesthetic that can handle everything from enterprise to start-ups.
  • Experience with:
  • Understanding wireframes, storyboards and site maps
  • Brand and logo mark design
  • Vector and raster-based design
  • Interface design (web and/or app)
  • Responsive design (desktop through mobile, hi-DPI applications)
  • Designing for content management systems and limitations (i.e. WordPress)

Nice-to-haves:

  • Experience with:
  • Print deliverables and processes
  • HTML, CSS
  • Web browser developer tools (Chrome Timeline, etc.)
  • Animation (desktop, video and/or browser-based)

If this sounds like you, please email us at team@braveux.com.
