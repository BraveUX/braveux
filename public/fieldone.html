Asset management application

We made FieldOne’s asset management web app easy to use and easy on the eyes.

Challenge

Make it easy to use.

FieldOne’s asset management product needed a UX overhaul, including a easier interface with an updated design.

Solution

Revamp the UX.

The Brave team designed and built the design/user interface for Field One’s core product, including a number of demonstrable improvements for the end user.

Scope

  • Information Architecture
  • Interface Design
  • Front End Development

Tracking that makes sense and saves time.

The core users of the product, dispatchers, wanted a product that would make it easier to view and interact with data on the screen. And that’s exactly what we delivered.

Coordination from factory to awarehouse

Simplified view that shows high-level availability of assets and resources.

Right place, right time

Created “scheduler assistant” functionality that relies on natural language (since users prefer not to be addressed like robots) and lets a dispatcher set recurring appointments.

Finding what matters most

Improved hierarchy of information that allows dispatchers to quickly identify and prioritize requests, tasks, and resources.

Functions that make sense

Reconfigured the core controls into simpler groupings, pairing similar functions to reduce the number of options/decision points for the user (*fewer options leads to improved efficiency).

“Brave helped us turn our application from dated and clunky to modern and easy. Our customers love the changes, and so do we.”

Daniel Gittler, FieldOne’s Director of Enterprise Services
